Understanding Crime Categories

This section provides an in-depth analysis of various crime categories against women, including Rape, Dowry Deaths (DD), Assault on Women (AoW), and Assault on Men (AoM). Each graph presents the average number of cases over recent years, highlighting the trends and the need for targeted interventions.

Average Rape Cases

The average number of rape cases reported shows a concerning trend over the years, indicating a persistent issue that requires urgent attention and action from law enforcement and society.

Average Dowry Death Cases

Dowry deaths represent a significant social issue, reflecting deep-rooted cultural norms that need to be challenged. Awareness and stricter legal measures can help mitigate this crime.

Average Assault on Women Cases

The data reveals the alarming frequency of assaults on women, emphasizing the need for improved safety measures and community support systems to protect vulnerable individuals.

Average Assault on Men Cases

While the focus is often on crimes against women, it's important to also consider the statistics for assaults on men, which highlights broader societal issues and the need for a holistic approach to crime prevention.
