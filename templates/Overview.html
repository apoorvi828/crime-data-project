Key Insights & Analysis

Crime Trends

  • Significant increase in cyber crimes against women
  • Domestic violence cases saw a spike during the COVID-19 pandemic
  • Gradual decrease in dowry-related crimes over the past decade

State-wise Insights

  • UP leads in overall crimes but has seen a drop in reported cases of dowry deaths
  • Assam's high crime rate is largely due to the increase in harassment and assault cases
  • Southern states like Kerala and Tamil Nadu report fewer crimes but higher conviction rates
